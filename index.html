Frida Kahlo

“Painting completed my life. I lost three children and a series of other things that would have fulfilled my horrible life. My painting took the place of all of this. I think work is the best.”

The artist

Fria Kahlo was a Mexican painter known for her many portraits, self-portraits, and works inspired by the nature and artifacts of Mexico. She started painting after being impaled in a bus accident at age 18. Kahlo later became politically active and married fellow communist artist Diego Rivera. She’s admired today as a feminist icon.
